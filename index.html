:CHOCOLATE CAKE RECIPE:

Here's a simple and delicious recipe for an easy chocolate cake:

INGREDIENTS:

  1. 2 cups all purpose Flour.
  2. 2 cups sugar.
  3. 3/4 cup cocoa powder.
    4. b
  4. 1/2 Tsp baking powder.
  5. 1/2 Tsp baking soda.
  6. 1 Tsp salt.
  7. 2 eggs.
  8. 1 cup evaporated milk.
  9. 1/2 cup oil.
  10. 1 Tsp vanilla.
  11. 1 cup boiling water
  12. 1 Tbsp coffee.

INSTRUCTIONS:

  • Just mix the separately the wet and dry ingredients.
  • If you don't have oven so just steam it for about 30-45 minutes depends on the size of your molder.

TAKE A NOTE PLEASE:

First, before you pour the mix ingredients in the molder, makes sure you do the dusting of flour in the molder but first brush some oil or butter on the molder.
